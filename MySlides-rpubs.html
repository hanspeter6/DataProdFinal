Cheddar Cheeses and the Acids That Make Them

Hans-Peter Bakker: 16 December 2016:

Setting the Scene

30 samples of cheddar cheese from the LaTrobe Valley of Victoria, Australia, were selected and analyzed for their chemical composition, in particular the concentrations of Acetic Acid, Hydrogen Sulphide and Lactic Acid. They were then subjected to taste tests, with an overall taste score obtained by combining the scores from several tasters.
The purpose of this app is to aid in the initial exploration of the relationships between taste and the concentration of the three acids.
